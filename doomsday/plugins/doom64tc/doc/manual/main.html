Getting Started

DooM64: the Absolution requires  a copy of the Doom2, TNT, or Plutonia iwad. (Do not ask for it, you will not get it.)

Minimum requirements: Doomsday:

  • 400 MHZ or better processor.
  • A 3dfx voodoo card with direct 3d or opengl support or better.
  • 32 megs of RAM.
